Welcome to Districtr! This is a free community webtool for redistricting and community mapping provided by the MGGG Redistricting Lab at Tufts University. We welcome your questions about Districtr and the maps that you create of the City of Napa, Napa County, and Napa Valley Unified School District: contact@mggg.org

Election Reform in Napa

  • The City of Napa is switching from electing city council seats at-large, to individual districts each with one or more members.
  • The Napa Valley Unified School District is considering switching from electing the Board of Education trustees at-large, to seven districts each with one member.
This districting process should be open and transparent for everyone, and the chosen district should represent neighborhoods and communities.

Try Drawing Districts

We invite you to use Districtr to draw your own districts and think about some of the tradeoffs for yourself. You can build 4 City Council Districts, 5 City Council Districts, or 7 City Council Districts using census blocks as the basic building blocks.
