Welcome to Districtr! This is a free community webtool for redistricting and community mapping provided by the MGGG Redistricting Lab at Tufts University. We welcome questions and inquiries about the tool and our work: contact@mggg.org

Try Drawing Districts

We invite you to use Districtr to draw your own districts and think about some of the tradeoffs for yourself. You can build 6 City Council Districts, or 11 City Council Districts using census blocks as the basic building blocks.
