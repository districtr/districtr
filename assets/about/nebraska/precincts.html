This shapefile was obtained from state and county sources and processed by students in Diana Davis' Districting Data REU. This effort was led by Poonam Sahoo and included Jiahua Chen, Kevin Choi, Keegan McKenna, Celia Parts, Justin Snyder and Alicia Yang. Additional processing was done by MGGG staff.
Raw data for Nebraska's precinct boundaries were sourced from NebraskaMAP, a GIS service of the State of Nebraska, and supplemented by more recent data from county officials. Election data were provided by the Nebraska Secretary of State, Elections Division. 2010 Decennial Census demographic data were downloaded from the Census API.
